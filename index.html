The 2020 Global Sulphur Regulations

From 1 January 2020 there is new global limit for Sulphur in fuel oil used on board ships in operation outside the current designated emission control areas. This means that the limit for Sulphur in fuel will be reduced to 0.5% against the current 3.5%.
